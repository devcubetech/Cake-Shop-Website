Our Sweet Journey

Founded in 2010, Blissful Bakes began as a passion project in a cozy home kitchen. Our founder, Isabella Rossi, combined her love for baking with her background in fine arts to create cakes that were not just desserts, but edible masterpieces.

Today, our boutique bakery in the heart of the city continues this tradition of excellence. Each cake is a labor of love, crafted with premium ingredients, seasonal flavors, and meticulous attention to detail.
